Local Processing & Security Principles

Chat files are read and analyzed locally in your browser. If “Remember uploads” is enabled, parsed data is stored locally (IndexedDB) to restore on refresh. This viewer does not intentionally upload chat content, but your deployment may load external scripts (e.g., analytics) depending on your HTML setup.
